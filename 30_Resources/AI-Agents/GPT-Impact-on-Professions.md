## Core Observation

In 2023, GPT emerged and rapidly developed within one to two years, essentially achieving the "holy grail" of natural language processing (自然语言处理). Not only did it solve translation tasks, but it also incidentally solved the "knowledge graph" (知识图谱) problem. The knowledge graph field once had a set of profound but not very practical methodologies, yet GPT solved the entire problem effortlessly.

## Industry Impact

### Translation Industry (翻译行业)

Since GPT's emergence, many language professionals have been "washed out" of socialized mass production (被洗出了社会化大生产). Most translation industry practitioners are moving toward the downward branch of the K-curve (K曲线往下方的那一支).

### Potential Expansion

Because GPT has also provided an almost perfect methodological path for solving knowledge graph tasks, this "washing out" of practitioners is likely to rapidly expand to other industries, including but not limited to:

- **Lawyers** (律师)
- **Accountants** (会计)
- **Auditors** (审计)
- **Internal Medicine Doctors** (内科医生)
- **Artists/Illustrators** (画师)

## Key Insight

GPT's breakthrough represents more than just solving individual tasks—it provides a unified methodological approach that can be applied across multiple knowledge-intensive professions. This creates a fundamental shift in how knowledge work is performed and who can perform it.
