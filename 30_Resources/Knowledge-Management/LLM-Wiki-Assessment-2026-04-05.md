# LLM Wiki Assessment

This note is a historical assessment of the LLM wiki pattern.

## Main Takeaway

The starter should behave less like a passive notes archive and more like a maintained wiki:

- raw sources stay traceable
- notes are updated instead of abandoned
- synthesis accumulates
- logs and linting keep the system coherent
