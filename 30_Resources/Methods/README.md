## What Are Method Cards?

Method cards are concise, actionable documents that capture **how you do things** - step-by-step procedures, workflows, and approaches you've discovered and want to reuse.

They differ from other Resources:
- **Resources** = "what I know" (reference knowledge, articles, research)
- **Methods** = "how I do things" (actionable procedures with clear steps)

## When to Create a Method Card

Create one when you discover a workflow or approach that:
- Saves significant time or effort
- You'll likely forget the exact steps
- Could be reused in the future
- Is unique to your setup (not easily Googleable)

## Method Card Template

```markdown
---
title: Method Name
date: YYYY-MM-DD
tags: [method, category]
trigger: "search phrase 1", "search phrase 2"
---

# Method Name

## When to Use (何时使用)

Brief description of the scenario where this method applies.

## Steps (步骤)

1. Step one
2. Step two
3. ...

## Tips (提示)

- Helpful tips or gotchas

## Related (相关)

- [[link-to-detailed-doc]] (for deeper reference)
```

The `trigger` field lists natural-language phrases you might use when searching. This helps AI retrieval.
