# Quick PPT with NotebookLM (用NotebookLM快速制作PPT)

## When to Use (何时使用)

When you need a complete, polished presentation in under 5 minutes with minimal manual editing.

## Steps (步骤)

1. Open Google NotebookLM and create a new notebook
2. Upload your source materials (documents, notes, URLs) as sources
3. Ask NotebookLM to generate a presentation outline from the sources
4. Review and adjust the generated outline
5. Export or copy the structured content into your preferred PPT tool
6. Make final visual adjustments if needed

## Tips (提示)

- The more relevant your source materials, the better the output
- NotebookLM works best when sources are focused on a single topic
- Review the generated content for accuracy before presenting
- This workflow is best for content-heavy presentations, not highly visual/design-driven ones
