# Routing and Naming Rules

## Route by Meaning

- `00_Inbox` for raw capture
- `10_Projects` for active outcomes
- `20_Areas` for ongoing responsibilities
- `30_Resources` for references and source material
- `40_Archive` for inactive material

## Name Files Clearly

Prefer names that tell future you what the file is for.

Good examples:

- `Weekly-Review-Template.md`
- `Writing-Project-Template.md`
- `AI-Chat-Distillation-Prompt.md`

Avoid:

- `notes.md`
- `misc.md`
- `new idea.md`

## Rename Slowly

Do not rename large parts of the system until your actual workflow justifies it.
