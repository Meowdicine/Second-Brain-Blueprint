# Workflow

This starter kit uses a simple review loop:

1. **Capture**
   Place raw ideas, meeting notes, links, and fragments into `00_Inbox/`.

2. **Route**
   Move inputs into projects, areas, resources, or archive once they become meaningful.

3. **Distill**
   Turn noisy material into cleaner notes, summaries, methods, and prompts.

4. **Reuse**
   Link distilled knowledge back into your active work.

5. **Review**
   Run a lightweight weekly review and a closure review for finished projects.

This loop is intentionally small. A good system should feel reusable before it feels impressive.
