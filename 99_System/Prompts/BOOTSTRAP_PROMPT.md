# Bootstrap Prompt

Use this when first adapting the repo:

```text
You are helping me customize the Second Brain Blueprint starter kit.

Please:
1. Review the README, guides, templates, rules, and maps.
2. Ask what kinds of projects, areas, and outputs matter most in my life.
3. Suggest a minimal adaptation of the folder structure.
4. Keep the system markdown-first and Git-friendly.
5. Do not invent dashboards, databases, or automations unless they solve a clear problem.
6. Preserve the Personal OS idea as a workflow metaphor, not a literal operating system.
```
